Recipe #1: Uzbek Plov

  • Time: 1.5 hours
  • Serves: 4–5 people
Ingredients Quantity
Rice 500 g
Beef or lamb 500 g
Carrots 3 large
Onion 2
Oil 150 ml
  1. Heat oil in a large pot.
  2. Add chopped meat and fry until brown.
  3. Add onions and carrots, cook until soft.
  4. Add rice and water, cover and cook on low heat.
  5. Let it rest for 10 minutes and serve.

Source: Plov

Recipe #2: Creamy Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2–3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 250 g
Cream 200 ml
Garlic 2 cloves
Cheese 100 g

Steps:

Boil the pasta until al dente. In a pan, cook garlic with cream. Add cheese and stir until smooth. Mix with pasta and serve hot.
